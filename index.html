Atelier of Axom is a heritage-driven platform committed to preserving, celebrating, and sharing the rich culture, craftsmanship, and timeless legacies of Assam with the world.

ABOUT US

More Than a Marketplace,
A Movement.

Atelier of Axom is a dedicated platform that brings together genuine Assamese artisans and conscious customers. We exist to bridge the gap between traditional craftsmanship and modern appreciation, creating opportunities for artisans to thrive while keeping our heritage alive for future generations.

Who We Are

We are a community of passionate individuals, storytellers, and culture lovers working tirelessly to empower Assamese artisans and promote the incredible diversity of our heritage.

What We Do

We connect skilled artisans with customers by offering a curated range of authentic handmade products that reflect the true essence of Assamese art and culture.

Why We Exist

To preserve traditional art forms, support artisan communities, and ensure that the cultural legacy of Assam is celebrated today and for generations to come.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted global platform for Assamese heritage, empowering artisans and inspiring the world through authentic craftsmanship and meaningful stories.

Our Commitment

We are committed to authenticity, fair trade, sustainability, and the holistic empowerment of artisan communities. Every product carries a story, a tradition, and a legacy we are proud to protect and share.
